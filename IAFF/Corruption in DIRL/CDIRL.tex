The following definition replaces the original Definition 5 (it is strictly more general).

\#Definition 1

Consider a meta-universe $\upsilon=(\mu,r)$ and $\beta:(0,\infty)\rightarrow(0,\infty)$. An autonomous $\Adi$-metapolicy $\alpha$ is called *$\beta$-rational for $\upsilon$* when there exists $\{L^\alpha_t: \HD{\bar{\mu}[\alpha]} \times \A \rightarrow [0,\infty]\}_{t \in (0,\infty)}$ s.t.

i. For any $h \in \HD{\bar{\mu}[\alpha]}$ and $t \in (0,\infty)$, there is $a \in \A$ s.t. $L^\alpha_t(ha)=0$.

ii. $\alpha_t(h)(a)=\exp(-\beta(t)L^\alpha_t(ha)) \max_{a^* \in \A} \alpha_t(h)(a^*)$

iii. For any $\pi \in \Pi_\Adi$ and $t \in (0,\infty)$

$$\lim_{t \rightarrow \infty}\min(\E{x\sim\mu_t\bowtie\pi}[\sum_{n=0}^\infty e^{-n/t} L^\alpha_t(x_{:n+1/2})]-\E{x\sim\mu_t\bowtie\pi}[\sum_{n=0}^\infty e^{-n/t}(\V^\upsilon_t(\underline{x_{:n}})-\Q^\upsilon_t(\underline{x_{:n+1/2}}))],0)=0$$



***

In condition ii, $\exp(-\infty)$ is understood to mean 0. A notable special case of condition iii is when for any $x \in \Adih$

$$\sum_{n=0}^\infty e^{-n/t} L^\alpha_t(x_{:n+1/2}) \geq \sum_{n=0}^\infty e^{-n/t}(\V^\upsilon_t(\underline{x_{:n}})-\Q^\upsilon_t(\underline{x_{:n+1/2}}))$$
